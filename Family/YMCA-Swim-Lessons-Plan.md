## Decision Framework

### Choose Membership IF:
- ✅ You or your wife would use the gym 2-3x/week
- ✅ You want family pool time beyond lessons
- ✅ Your son might do other programs (basketball, soccer, summer camp)
- ✅ You need childcare while working out
- ✅ You want guaranteed early registration for popular time slots

### Choose Pay-Per-Session IF:
- ✅ You're ONLY doing swim lessons (nothing else)
- ✅ You're okay with late registration (may not get preferred times)
- ✅ Budget is tight and you won't use other amenities
