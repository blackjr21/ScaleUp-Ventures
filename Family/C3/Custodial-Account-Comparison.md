## Tax & Financial Aid Considerations

**Tax Treatment (2025):**
- First $1,350 of unearned income: Tax-free
- Next $1,350: Taxed at child's rate (~10%)
- Above $2,700: Taxed at parent's rate ("kiddie tax")

**Financial Aid Impact:**
- Assessed at 20% (vs. 5.64% for parent assets)
- Consider 529 plans for college-specific savings

**Gift Tax Limits:**
- $19,000/year per person ($38,000 for married couples)
- Unlikely to affect $50 starting investments
